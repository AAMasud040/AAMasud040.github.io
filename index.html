About Me

I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from United International University (UIU), having earned my BSE (2018-2023) in Computer Science and Engineering. Throughout my academic journey, I have cultivated a fervent passion for robotics and artificial intelligence.

My path has been marked by significant achievements, including my leadership role within the Software and Autonomous team of the highly competitive UIU Mars Rover Team. Additionally, I developed the GUIs and the rover's current Autonomous framework, which played a pivotal role in achieving success in prestigious international competitions such as the University Rover Challenge and Anatolian Rover Challenge.

In the realm of research, my expertise spans diverse fields, encompassing Meta-Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision.

I am actively seeking admission to a funded PhD program in robotics and computer vision. My vision extends beyond personal growth; I aim to leverage this opportunity to establish a cross-country robotics lab in Bangladesh. This envisioned lab will serve as a hub of innovation and learning, empowering students from across the country to thrive and explore the limitless possibilities of robotics and artificial intelligence. And I aspire to turn this dream into a reality.
